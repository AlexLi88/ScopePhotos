What is Scope

Scope creates dynamic albums, aggregated with social images by location/time/topinc, allowing users to search and broadcast across platforms.

What is the difference between a Public scope and a Private scope?

Public scopes: Everyone and anyone can see this album! Anyone present at the event/location can shoot photos directly into the event or upload photos from their camera roll. When you set up this scope, you must set a location so others can upload into the scope.

Public scopes + Members: This is exactly the same as a regular Public scope, except you can invite friends to upload their photos into it as well! Everyone can see this album, but only invited members can shoot directly into the event or upload photos from their camera roll.

Private scope: Private scopes are for your eyes only! Simply shoot photos into this scope or upload them from the camera roll.

Private scope + Members: Want to post a photo that your mom shouldn’t see? Private scopes with members offer you the unique opportunity to share photos with select friends!

How does the map feature work on Scope?

The map feature gives you the power to find photos posted across major social media platforms from anywhere in the world. For example, have you always wanted to go to Times Square in New York City? Simply go to the map page, type in the name, and press get photos! You will see all photos posted to social media from that specific spot.

How do I find directions to photos?

1. Select the image you like.
2. Click the location button on the top right hand corner of the screen.
3. Click the photo on the map.
4. Select Get Photos!

Can I share a scope to other social media platforms?

Yes! Open any scope and tap the share button in the top right corner of the screen. You can share the scope link by text, email, and other social media platforms.

How do I hide photos in my scopes?

With Scope’s moderating feature, hiding photos only takes four easy steps:
1. Open your scope
2. Press Edit
3. Press Moderate photos
4. Select all the photos you want to hide

Experiencing and sharing live events from around the world has never been easier! By pulling images from across social media platforms, you can now fully understand, grasp, and experience everything there is to know about your favourite celebrities, events, cities, and bloggers

How do I recommend Scope to my friends?

Great question!
1. Simply go to your profile
2. Click the settings icon in the top right hand corner of the screen
3. Select “Recommend Scope to Friends”
4. Choose between text message, email, social media, and more!
